Be Part of My Greedy Algorithm! Inspired by the algorithms designed to keep us on our devices in a state popularly known as doomscrolling, I’ve started creating my own. Reflecting upon what elements determine whether a post successfully keeps me hooked, I’ve identified three key factors: • Time spent looking at the post • Time taken to react to the post (I’ve concluded that liking or disliking makes no difference) • A big penalty if I choose to exit the site on that post This system calculates the score of every post, and the highest-ranking ones will appear on the leaderboard above. Now, what I need is to be fed with more and more effective posts, with the end goal of scrolling until the day I die. For that, I’ll rely on human labor, meaning you, to compete by submitting posts that hopefully rank high on the leaderboard. As algorithms do, you are encouraged to use the current leaderboard to guide your decisions on what type of post will succeed. I will apply any reasonable suggestions to further refine my algorithm and would also love your feedback. Mail: galerie.ingebjorg@outlook.com
